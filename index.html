Acuity Finance

Financial Acuity, Sharp Insights for Smarter Wealth

About the App

Acuity Finance is a comprehensive personal finance application designed to help you track expenses, manage savings, and gain crystal-clear insights into your financial health. Empowering you to make smarter decisions effortlessly.

Key Features

  • AI Receipt Scanner Extract details from receipts using Gemini AI seamlessly.
  • Budgets & AI Analyzer Set spending limits and get actionable financial advice.
  • Google Drive Sync Securely backup and restore multiple accounting databases.
  • Debts & Receivables Keep accurate track of money you owe or is owed to you.
  • Recurring Transactions Automate repeating financial activities like subscriptions.
  • 113k+ Category Icons Massive built-in library for highly customized categories.
  • Multiple Wallets Manage cash, bank, and e-wallets seamlessly in one app.
  • Calendar & Stats Visualize your daily cash flow and monthly reports easily.
  • Local First Database Blazing fast offline performance via local Isar database.
  • Customizable UI Beautiful Dark & Light mode with custom accent colors.
