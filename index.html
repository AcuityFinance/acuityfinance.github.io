About the App

Acuity Finance is a comprehensive personal finance and budget management application designed to help you track your expenses, manage your savings, and gain crystal-clear insights into your financial health. Our purpose is to empower individuals to make smarter, data-driven financial decisions effortlessly.

Key Features

  • Smart Budgeting Create and manage customized monthly budgets to stay on top of your finances.
  • Expense Tracking Categorize and monitor your daily transactions to see exactly where your money goes.
  • Financial Analytics Visualize your spending patterns with beautiful, easy-to-understand charts.
  • Privacy First Your financial data is securely stored locally on your device, ensuring complete privacy.
